## Our Story (verbatim substance, lightly reconstructed)
> Hyderabad Biryani House was born from a simple mission: to bring the true
> flavors of Hyderabad to Tampa.
>
> Our roots trace back to kitchens where the aromas of saffron, slow-cooked
> rice, tender meat, and hand-ground spices brought families together. When we
> opened in Tampa, we wanted to share the depth, warmth, and heritage behind
> every authentic Hyderabadi dish.
>
> Every item on our menu reflects recipes passed down through generations,
> prepared with traditional techniques and careful attention to detail — from
> slow-steaming our sealed biryanis (dum) to preparing curries fresh daily with
> authentically sourced spices. Heritage methods, modern refinement.
>
> More than a restaurant, HBH is a tribute to culture, a celebration of
> tradition, and an invitation for Tampa to experience Hyderabadi hospitality.
