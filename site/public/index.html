The ritual

Three things we never rush.

1

Marinate

Meat rests overnight in yogurt, saffron, and garam masala we grind ourselves. The clay oven does the rest.

2

Seal & steam

Rice cascades over the meat, the handi is capped with dough, and the dum begins. Nothing escapes but patience.

3

Open at the table

Low coals, slow steam, and the pot opens in front of you. That first lift of the lid is the whole point.
